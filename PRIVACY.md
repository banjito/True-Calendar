# Privacy Policy - True Calendar

**Last Updated:** November 7, 2025  
**Effective Date:** November 7, 2025

## Overview

True Calendar is a minimal calendar application designed to help you organize your schedule. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Calendar Events**: Event titles, descriptions, dates, times, and recurrence patterns that you manually enter into the app
- **Notification Preferences**: Your settings for event reminders and notifications

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Basic device type (iPhone/iPad) and iOS version for compatibility purposes
- **App Usage**: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance (no personal identifiers)

## How We Use Your Information

- **Core Functionality**: Store and display your calendar events locally on your device
- **Notifications**: Send you timely reminders for events you've created
- **App Improvement**: Analyze anonymous usage patterns to enhance user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage Only**: All your calendar data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
- **No Cloud Sync**: We do not sync your data to any cloud servers or third-party services
- **No User Accounts**: You do not need to create an account or provide personal information to use this app
- **Data Encryption**: Your data is encrypted on your device in accordance with iOS security standards

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your calendar data remains private and stored only on your device.

## Third-Party Services

This app may use the following third-party services:
- **Apple Push Notification Service**: For delivering event notifications (Apple's standard service)
- **Expo Development Platform**: For app development and distribution (does not access your personal data)

## Your Rights

- **Access**: You have full access to all your calendar data within the app
- **Deletion**: You can delete individual events or remove all app data through iOS Settings
- **Correction**: You can edit any event information at any time
- **Data Portability**: Your data is stored locally and can be accessed through the app interface

## Data Retention

- Your calendar data is retained on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
- When you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed

## Children's Privacy

True Calendar does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

## Legal Basis for Processing

Under applicable privacy laws, we process your data based on:

## International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not use cloud services, no international data transfers occur.
